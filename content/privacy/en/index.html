What data we collect

When you submit the contact form, we collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone or WhatsApp number (if provided)

  • Any message you include

We do not use analytics cookies or advertising trackers on this site.

Purpose and legal basis

We process your data to respond to your enquiry and introduce you to a suitable estate agency at your request.

Legal basis (GDPR Art. 6(1)(b)): processing is necessary to take steps at your request prior to entering into a contract — namely, arranging your introduction to a partner agency.

We do not use automated decision-making or profiling.

Who receives your data

Your details are shared only with the one partner agency selected for your introduction, and only with your knowledge. We do not sell your data.

Our partner agencies are:

  • Marbella-Resales
  • Marbella For Sale
  • Orangestate Properties
  • Resales Online network members (as applicable to your search)

Data processors

Formspree Inc. (United States) processes contact form submissions on our behalf. Formspree stores and forwards your message to us. Where data is transferred outside the EEA, Formspree relies on Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission. See Formspree's privacy policy.

Cloudflare Inc. hosts and delivers this website. Cloudflare may process technical connection data (such as IP address and request logs) as part of providing CDN and security services. See Cloudflare's privacy policy.

Retention

Enquiry data is retained only for as long as needed to complete your introduction and any reasonable follow-up, then deleted. We do not keep contact form data indefinitely.

Your rights

Under the GDPR you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, data portability, and to object. To exercise any of these rights, email info@agg.homes.

You may lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority:

  • Spain: Agencia Española de Protección de Datos (AEPD) — aepd.es
  • Netherlands: Autoriteit Persoonsgegevens (AP) — autoriteitpersoonsgegevens.nl

Cookies

This site does not set non-essential cookies. Cookie consent is handled separately on the main site where applicable.
