Privacy policy

Last updated: 14 June 2026

This policy explains how A. Gonzalez (trading as AGG.homes) processes personal data when you use this website or submit the contact form. AGG.homes is an independent property introduction service based in Marbella, Spain.

What data we collect

When you submit the contact form, we collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone or WhatsApp number (if provided)
  • Preferred area and budget range (if selected)
  • Any message you choose to include

We do not use analytics cookies or third-party advertising trackers on this site.

Why we process your data

We process your data to:

  • Respond to your enquiry and introduce you to a suitable estate agency
  • Follow up on your property search at your request

Legal basis (GDPR): performance of steps at your request prior to a contract (Article 6(1)(b)), and where applicable your consent when you submit the form (Article 6(1)(a)).

Who receives your data

Your details are shared only with the estate agency we introduce you to, and only with your knowledge. We do not sell your data.

Form submissions are transmitted via Formspree (Formspree, Inc., USA), which acts as a data processor on our behalf. Formspree provides a Data Processing Agreement and processes data under standard contractual safeguards. See Formspree's privacy policy.

Our website is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. Cloudflare may process technical connection data (such as IP address) as part of delivering the site securely. We use only strictly necessary infrastructure cookies where required for security and delivery.

Retention

Enquiry data is kept for as long as needed to handle your introduction and any follow-up, and for up to two years thereafter unless a longer period is required by law or you ask us to delete it sooner.

Your rights

Under the GDPR you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict or object to processing, and to data portability where applicable. You may also lodge a complaint with the Spanish data protection authority (AEPD) or your local EU supervisory authority.

To exercise your rights, contact info@agg.homes.

Cookies

This site does not set non-essential cookies. If that changes, we will update this policy and request consent where required.
